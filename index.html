The Salty Dog Cafe

Description :

A cafe named "Salty Dog Cafe" was established by the Townspeople in 1987 in honor of Jake's heroic feat.

How was the cafe Built :

The Salty Dog brand stems from the mythical story about a dog named Jake who rescued his master,
John Braddock after their fishing boat, the Salty Dog,
was sunk by a sudden storm off the shores of Hilton Head island.
And so, the Towns People built the Cafe in honor of Jake.

What does it serves

The "Salty dog Cafe" is very Popular. The Cafe serves
Breakfast such as Egg, Pancake, Waffles, and drinks like Cofee, Tea, Water,
and other refreshments. They also serve Lunch and Dinner.
